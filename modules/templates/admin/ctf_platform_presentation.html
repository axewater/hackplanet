Hacking Labs

Realistic, Azure-hosted environments for hands-on practice. Accessible via VPN, these labs provide a safe space to apply ethical hacking techniques without risking real systems.

Challenges

A variety of cybersecurity challenges designed to test and improve skills in different areas of ethical hacking. Challenges are regularly updated to keep content fresh and relevant.

Study Rooms

Dedicated spaces for learning, featuring curated resources, tutorials, and documentation to support skill development in ethical hacking and cybersecurity.

Quizzes

Interactive quizzes to test knowledge and reinforce learning. Quizzes cover various topics in cybersecurity and ethical hacking, helping employees identify areas for improvement.

Leaderboard

A competitive element that motivates employees to improve their skills. The leaderboard showcases top performers, encouraging friendly competition and continuous learning.

User Progress Tracking

Detailed progress tracking for both employees and managers. This feature helps identify strengths and areas for improvement, facilitating targeted skill development.
