How Scoring Works

Points are awarded for various achievements across the platform:

Point Sources

  • Lab machine flags (user & root)

  • Special events

Leaderboard Features

  • Overall Score Rankings
  • Customizable Avatar Display
  • Real-time Updates

Profile Customization

You can customize your avatar that appears on the leaderboard by:

  • Selecting from the gallery of pre-made avatars
  • Uploading your own custom avatar image
  • Managing your profile settings in the Profile section
